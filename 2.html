Gemini

Gemini, developed by OpenAI, refers to a family of advanced AI models designed to handle various tasks across text, images, and more. It combines capabilities of language understanding and generation (like ChatGPT) with multi-modal functionalities, enabling interaction with text and other formats such as images or even code. Gemini models are designed for flexibility, offering enhanced creative, problem-solving, and analytical abilities across diverse applications. This suite represents OpenAI's approach to pushing the boundaries of AI's potential in both personal and professional domains.
